Privacy Policy – HoloScan

Effective Date: May 29, 2025

1. General Information

HoloScan is a mobile application designed to help users catalogue and manage their personal collection of Star Wars Unlimited trading cards. The app does not require login or any personal data input.

2. Data Collected

The app only collects data about the user's card collection, such as card names, quantities, conditions, and any other notes the user may choose to input manually.

3. No Personal Data

HoloScan does not collect any personal data such as name, email address, phone number, GPS location, device data, or media files.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted to any external servers or cloud services. Application's developer doesn't have access to any user data.

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared, sold, or transferred to third parties.

6. Tracking Technologies

The app does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics tools, or any similar technologies.

7. Data Deletion

Users can delete all stored data directly through the settings section of the app.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
